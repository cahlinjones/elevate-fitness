About Elevate FitX

More than a gym - we're a community dedicated to your success

Our Story

At Elevate FitX, we take immense pride in being different from other gyms and fitness centers. We're not just a place to work out - we're a community dedicated to helping you achieve your goals and become the best version of yourself.

Founded with a vision to create an inclusive, supportive fitness environment in Pocatello, Idaho, Elevate FitX has grown to become the area's premier full-service fitness center. We believe that everyone deserves access to quality fitness facilities and expert guidance, which is why we've eliminated tier systems and made all our amenities available to every member.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to help every person who walks through our doors feel comfortable, work hard, have fun, and leave feeling elevated. Whether you're here for cardio, weightlifting, swimming, group fitness classes, or just to play some racquetball or basketball, we're committed to supporting your journey every step of the way.

What Makes Us Different

We're proud to accept all major insurance fitness programs, including SilverSneakers, making fitness accessible and affordable for everyone in our community. Your membership includes full access to all our facilities - no hidden fees, no tier restrictions, just unlimited potential to achieve your goals.

As part of our fit family, you'll be immersed in a culture that celebrates everyone's success. We guarantee that with your consistent effort, you'll feel comfortable, work hard, have fun, and most importantly - leave feeling elevated!

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Community First

We believe in the power of community support and celebrate each member's achievements together.

Everyone is welcome here, regardless of age, fitness level, or background. We're here for you.
